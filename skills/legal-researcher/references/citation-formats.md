# Australian Legal Citation Formats (AGLC 4th Edition)

## Overview

The Australian Guide to Legal Citation (AGLC) 4th edition is the standard for legal citation in Australia. This guide covers the most common citation formats for legislation and case law.

## Neutral Citations

### What is a Neutral Citation?

A medium-neutral citation (or neutral citation) is court-assigned and doesn't depend on which law report series publishes the case.

**Format:** [Year] Court Identifier Decision Number

**Examples:**
- [2024] HCA 15 (High Court of Australia, 15th decision of 2024)
- [2023] NSWCA 245 (NSW Court of Appeal, 245th decision of 2023)
- [2024] FCA 89 (Federal Court of Australia, 89th decision of 2024)

### Court Identifiers

**Commonwealth Courts:**
- HCA - High Court of Australia
- FCA - Federal Court of Australia (single judge or Full Court)
- FCAFC - Federal Court of Australia, Full Court
- FedCFamC - Federal Circuit and Family Court

**NSW Courts:**
- NSWSC - Supreme Court
- NSWCA - Court of Appeal
- NSWCCA - Court of Criminal Appeal
- NSWDC - District Court

**Victorian Courts:**
- VSC - Supreme Court (including Court of Appeal)
- VSCA - Court of Appeal
- VCC - County Court

**Other States:**
- QSC, QCA - Queensland
- SASCFC, SASC - South Australia
- WASC, WASCA - Western Australia
- TASSC, TASCCA - Tasmania

**Territories:**
- ACTSC, ACTCA - ACT
- NTSC, NTCA - Northern Territory

## Case Law Citation

### Full Citation Format

**Basic Format:**
*Case Name* (Year) Volume Reporter First Page, Pinpoint

**Example:**
*Donoghue v Stevenson* [1932] AC 562, 580

### Case Names

**Parties:**
- Italicize case names: *Smith v Jones*
- Use 'v' (not 'vs' or 'versus')
- Only first plaintiff and first defendant
- Use short names where appropriate: *Commissioner of Taxation* becomes *FCT*

**Common Abbreviations:**
- R v (Regina/Rex v - criminal cases)
- Ex parte (application by one party)
- Re (In the matter of)
- FCT (Federal Commissioner of Taxation)
- DPP (Director of Public Prosecutions)
- ASIC (Australian Securities and Investments Commission)

### Law Reports

**Authorized Reports (preferred):**
- CLR - Commonwealth Law Reports (High Court)
- FCR - Federal Court Reports
- ALR - Australian Law Reports
- NSWLR - NSW Law Reports
- VR - Victorian Reports

**Other Reports:**
- FLR - Federal Law Reports
- ACSR - Australian Corporations and Securities Reports
- FamLR - Family Law Reports

### Pinpoint References

**Refer to specific pages or paragraphs:**

**Page References:**
*Smith v Jones* (2020) 150 CLR 100, 115 (page 115)

**Paragraph References (with neutral citations):**
*Smith v Jones* [2020] HCA 15 at [42] (paragraph 42)

**Multiple References:**
*Smith v Jones* [2020] HCA 15 at [42], [58]–[60]

### Judicial Officers

When citing specific judges:

**Format:**
*Case Name* [Citation] at [pinpoint] (Judge name)

**Examples:**
- *Smith v Jones* [2020] HCA 15 at [42] (Kiefel CJ)
- *Smith v Jones* [2020] HCA 15 at [100] (Gordon J, dissenting)

**Judicial Titles:**
- CJ - Chief Justice
- J - Justice
- JJ - Justices (plural)
- JA - Justice of Appeal

## Legislation Citation

### Commonwealth Acts

**Basic Format:**
*Act Title* Year (Jurisdiction)

**Examples:**
- *Corporations Act 2001* (Cth)
- *Income Tax Assessment Act 1936* (Cth)

**With Section:**
*Corporations Act 2001* (Cth) s 180(1)

**Multiple Sections:**
- Consecutive: ss 180–182
- Non-consecutive: ss 180, 185, 190

**Subdivisions:**
- s 51(xx) (section 51, paragraph xx)
- s 180(1)(a)(ii) (section 180, subsection 1, paragraph a, subparagraph ii)

### State and Territory Acts

**Format:** *Act Title* Year (State)

**State Abbreviations:**
- (NSW) - New South Wales
- (Vic) - Victoria
- (Qld) - Queensland
- (SA) - South Australia
- (WA) - Western Australia
- (Tas) - Tasmania
- (NT) - Northern Territory
- (ACT) - Australian Capital Territory

**Examples:**
- *Retail Leases Act 1994* (NSW)
- *Retail Leases Act 2003* (Vic)
- *Property Law Act 1974* (Qld)

### Short Titles

After first full citation, you may use short form:

**First mention:**
*Corporations Act 2001* (Cth) ('*Corporations Act*')

**Subsequent mentions:**
*Corporations Act* s 180

Or simply: s 180 (if context clear)

### Regulations and Subordinate Legislation

**Format:**
*Regulation Title* Year (Jurisdiction)

**Example:**
*Corporations Regulations 2001* (Cth) reg 2.1.01

**Note:** Use 'reg' for regulation, 'r' for rule, 'cl' for clause, 'sch' for schedule

## Full Citation Examples

### High Court Cases

**With authorized report:**
*Donoghue v Stevenson* [1932] AC 562

**With neutral citation:**
*Commonwealth v Australian Capital Territory* (2013) 250 CLR 441, [2013] HCA 55

**With pinpoint to judgment:**
*Commonwealth v Australian Capital Territory* (2013) 250 CLR 441, 455 [38] (French CJ)

### Federal Court Cases

*Australian Securities and Investments Commission v Healey* (2011) 196 FCR 291, [2011] FCA 717

### State Court Cases

### Criminal Cases

*R v Smith* [2023] NSWSC 145

*Director of Public Prosecutions v Jones* (2022) 60 VR 234

## Common Errors to Avoid

**1. Wrong Abbreviations:**
- ❌ *Smith vs Jones*
- ✅ *Smith v Jones*

**2. Missing Italics:**
- ❌ Smith v Jones
- ✅ *Smith v Jones*

**3. Incorrect Jurisdiction:**
- ❌ *Corporations Act 2001* (without jurisdiction)
- ✅ *Corporations Act 2001* (Cth)

**4. Wrong Section Notation:**
- ❌ *Act* section 42
- ✅ *Act* s 42

**5. Incorrect Pinpoint:**
- ❌ *Case* [2023] HCA 15 page 42
- ✅ *Case* [2023] HCA 15 at [42]

**6. Wrong Date Format:**
- ❌ *Case* 2023 HCA 15
- ✅ *Case* [2023] HCA 15

**7. Missing Court Identifier:**
- ❌ *Case* [2023] 15
- ✅ *Case* [2023] HCA 15

## Verification Citations

When documenting verification, include:

**For Cases:**
*Case Name* [Year] Court ID Number, URL: [full AustLII or court URL], verified [date]

**For Legislation:**
*Act Title* Year (Jurisdiction) s X, URL: [legislation database URL], as at [version date], verified [date]

**Example:**
*Commonwealth v Australian Capital Territory* (2013) 250 CLR 441, [2013] HCA 55, URL: www.austlii.edu.au/cgi-bin/viewdoc/au/cases/cth/HCA/2013/55.html, verified 4 February 2026

## Special Cases

### Unreported Judgments

*Smith v Jones* (Supreme Court of New South Wales, 15 March 2023, unreported)

Or with neutral citation:
*Smith v Jones* [2023] NSWSC 67 (unreported)

### Cases Under Appeal

Note if a case is subject to appeal:
*Smith v Jones* [2023] NSWSC 145 (on appeal to NSWCA)

### Affirmed or Overturned on Appeal

*Smith v Jones* [2023] NSWDC 45, affirmed [2024] NSWSC 89

*Smith v Jones* [2023] NSWSC 145, overturned [2024] NSWCA 78

### Legislation Pending Commencement

*Act Title* (Cth) (not yet in force)

Or: *Act Title* (Cth) (commences 1 July 2024)

## Online and Electronic Sources

## Best Practices

1. **Always verify** official citation from primary source
2. **Use neutral citations** where available (preferred in modern practice)
3. **Include pinpoints** for specific propositions
4. **Be consistent** in formatting throughout document
5. **Check jurisdiction** is correct for legislation
6. **Italicize case names** in all instances
7. **Use AGLC abbreviations** not full words
8. **Include verification** information for research purposes
9. **Note currency** (as at date) for legislation
10. **Document sources** (URLs, databases used)

By following AGLC standards, you ensure your citations are professional, consistent, and easily verifiable by readers.
